About Mindful Menu

Our mission

We make meal planning simple. We are mindful of your time (simple meals for the win!), health (healthy recipes that also taste great) and budget (no wasted food and an affordable subscription). We do the planning for you so you can spend more time with your family.

Each week you will receive a meal plan, shopping list and a specific prep schedule. Our weekly menu will reflect the changing seasons to keep produce prices affordable and your palette satisfied!

Mindful Menu is the simplified way to bring dinner to your table each night.
